About Me

Hello creatively I am known as "KHONTROL" I am a South Florida rapper who grew up in the Tampa, FL area up until my family relocated at the start of my freshman year of highschool. I was born with mild cerebral palsy (diplegia spastic) at birth meaning for most of my life up until junior year of high school (when I had my major surgery done) I had major mobility restrictions due to my condition. Mix the physical with a past of financial struggles music was just always an outlet and way to escape. Growing up around music since my father was a DJ my entire life up until recent years and surrounded by countless people aside from my father such as Supa Dups from Black Chiney Records, DJ Nano, and much more I always felt influenced to continue to pursue music.
